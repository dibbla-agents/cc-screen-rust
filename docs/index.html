▸ what it's for

Built for keeping a lot of agents on the go.

  1. 01

    A whole team, always on

    Run as many agents as you like, side by side, around the clock — Anthropic, Google, OpenAI, Kimi, you name it. They keep working whether you're watching or not.

  2. 02

    Check in from anywhere

    Open them on your phone or your laptop and jump straight into any one of them. Watching TV or heading to work — your agents are a tap away.

  3. 03

    Cowork on the files

    Browse, view, edit, and move files in and out — everything your agents are working on. It's a real working session, not just a chat box.

▸ getting started

Up and running in two steps.

① Start it on your computer

The machine where your coding agents already live.

$ curl --proto '=https' --tlsv1.2 -LsSf \
    .../cc-screen-rust-installer.sh | sh
$ cc-screen-rust install   # keeps it running in the background

Then open it in any browser and Add to Home Screen on your phone.

② Or use the native app

A fast desktop companion for Mac and Linux.

$ curl --proto '=https' --tlsv1.2 -LsSf \
    .../cc-screen-tui-installer.sh | sh
$ ccs --server http://<your-computer>:8839

Switch between agents, watch them work, and jump in any time.
